Frequently Asked Questions

To verify your identity with the IRS, you'll need to complete the following steps:

  • Provide accurate personal information (Step 1)
  • Answer security questions based on your records (Step 2)
  • Select a verification method and provide required details (Step 3)

After completing all three steps, your identity will be verified and you'll gain access to IRS online services.

If you're unable to verify your identity online, you have several options:

  • Contact IRS Support: Call 1-800-829-1040 for assistance with identity verification
  • Visit a Taxpayer Assistance Center: Schedule an in-person appointment at a local IRS office
  • Mail Verification: In some cases, you may be able to verify your identity by mail

Note: Identity verification is required for security purposes to protect your personal tax information.
